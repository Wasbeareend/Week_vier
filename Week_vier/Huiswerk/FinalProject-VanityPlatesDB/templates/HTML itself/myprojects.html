Youth Participation in democracy

For this project I worked with the municipality of Heerenveen. I did research about how young people can join and take part in local democracy.

I looked at things like: do young people know what the municipality does, do they feel heard and what stops them from joining talks or meetings.

I used a simple survey, some interviews and I read sme policy documents. In the end I wrote a short report with ideas for better youth participatio, like more clear information, using online tools and giving young people real influence
